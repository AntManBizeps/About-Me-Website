Travels

I’ve always had a passion for traveling. It allows me to explore new cultures, meet interesting people, and discover beautiful places. Traveling broadens my perspective, inspires creativity, and gives me a sense of adventure. Whether it's a weekend getaway or a long journey, every trip brings something unique and memorable.

Hiking

Hiking is one of my favorite activities because it allows me to connect with nature while challenging myself physically. Being out on a trail, surrounded by beautiful landscapes, gives me a sense of peace and accomplishment. It’s a great way to clear my mind, stay fit, and explore the outdoors.

Gym

I enjoy going to the gym as it helps me stay physically fit and improves my overall well-being. It’s a great way to push my limits, build strength, and relieve stress. Whether it’s lifting weights or doing calisthenics, I find the gym to be a motivating environment that helps me set and achieve personal goals while maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Dancing

Dancing is one of my greatest passions, and I love exploring different styles like discofox, hustle, rock 'n roll, and west coast swing. These dances allow me to express myself, stay active, and have fun. Whether it’s the fast-paced energy of rock 'n roll or the smooth, flowing movements of west coast swing, dancing brings joy and connection, and it’s a perfect way to unwind and socialize.

Running

It's a simple yet powerful activity that allows me to challenge myself, improve my endurance, and experience the outdoors in a different way.

In November, I’m aiming to complete the marathon in Athens. It’s been a long-term goal of mine, and I’m excited to take on this challenge. Training has been tough, but the idea of running in such a historic city keeps me motivated. I can’t wait to cross the finish line and accomplish this personal milestone!
